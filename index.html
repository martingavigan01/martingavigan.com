Hosting My Site January 9, 2018

Now I have a domain, I now need a place to host my site and content.
For this, Im using Amazon Web Services, the link is on the right. I found this page very useful. The page walks you through the steps needed to config "S3", which is a cloud storage space that will hold your sites content and Route 53, which connects your new domain name to the place where your content is stored. This means, once people enter your domain URL into a browner, they will sent to your content stored in S3. The one thing the link does tell you. Once you have completed the steps, you will need to copy the nameservers that are defined by Route 53 and save them against your domain name in GoDaddy. This YouTube tutorial explain how to do this, (it also includes the step on the link)
